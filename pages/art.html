God Is In The Radio

Constructed from a mess of Pinto parts, an iPad Mini, projectors and speakers, this set takes the passenger on a dark wintery drive through the city. The passenger can access different audio compisitions through the radio interface, each prompting introspection and philosophical queries.

The Joys & The Horrors

This black and white screenprinted accordian book explores growing up in rural Nova Scotia. The book includes a custom handmade box.

Hope Trike

Custom cargo-trike presented at a group show where participants can ride the tricycle infront of a rear-projected video that mimics cyclying along a rural road. Pedalling activates a dynamo light inside a modified overhead projector to illuminate a message only visible to the rider.
